Skoda Slavia

Skoda Slavia latest update Latest Update: Skoda has revised the Slavia prices and renamed its entire variant lineup. We have now detailed the variant-wise features of the 2024 Slavia. Price: The Skoda Slavia is now priced from Rs 10.69 lakh to Rs 18.69 lakh (ex-showroom pan-India), although only for a limited period. Variants: It is available in three broad variants: Classic, Signature, and Prestige. Colour Options: It is available in six monotone and two dual-tone colours: Lava Blue, Crystal Blue, Tornado Red, Carbon Steel, Brilliant Silver, Candy White, Crystal Blue with Carbon Steel roof, and Brilliant Silver with Carbon Steel roof. The Elegance edition is available in an exclusive Deep Black colour scheme. Boot Space: Skoda’s compact sedan has a boot space of 521 litres. Engine and Transmission: The Skoda Slavia gets two turbo-petrol engine options: 1-litre turbo-petrol engine (115 PS / 178 Nm) 1.5-litre turbo-petrol engine (150 PS / 250 Nm) Both models come with a 6-speed manual gearbox as standard, while the automatic versions include a 6-speed torque converter for the 1-litre engine and a 7-speed dual-clutch automatic gearbox (DCT) for the 1.5-litre engine. Claimed Fuel Efficiency: 1-litre MT: 20.32 kmpl 1-litre AT: 18.73 kmpl 1.5-litre MT: 19 kmpl 1.5-litre DCT: 19.36 kmpl The 1.5-litre engine features cylinder deactivation technology for improved fuel efficiency. Features: Key features include a 10-inch touchscreen, an 8-inch digital driver’s display, 8 speakers and a subwoofer, cruise control, automatic climate control, and a single-pane sunroof. It also gets a powered driver and co-driver seat and ventilated front seats. The Ambition Plus variant also includes a built-in dashcam. Safety: Safety features comprise six airbags, ISOFIX child-seat anchorages, a tyre pressure monitoring system (TPMS), a hill-hold assist, and a rear parking camera. Rivals: The Skoda Slavia competes with the Hyundai Verna, Maruti Ciaz, Honda City, and Volkswagen Virtus.
